Frequently Asked Questions

1. My app is crashing or freezing. What should I do?

Try the following steps:

  • Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed.
  • Restart your device.
  • Ensure your device is running the latest version of iOS or Android.

If the issue continues, please email us with your device model, operating system version, and a brief description of what happens.

3. How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled directly by the store where you purchased the app:

We are happy to help guide you through the process, but final decisions are made by Apple or Google.
